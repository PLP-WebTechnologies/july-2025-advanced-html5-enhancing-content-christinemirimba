Car Terminology

Horsepower (HP)
A measurement of engine power output, affecting a car's speed and acceleration.
Top Speed
The highest speed a car can achieve under optimal conditions.
Torque
The twisting force that helps a car accelerate from a standstill or climb steep hills.
SUV(Sport Utility Vehicle)
A type of car that combines features of passenger cars and off-road vehicles, offering a balance of comfort, space, and ruggedness.
Supercar
a high-performance sports car, often with exotic styling and limited production
